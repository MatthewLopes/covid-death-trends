COVID-19 Death Trends


COVID-19 is something we have unfortunately been dealing with since early 2020. The goal of this narrative visualization
is to better understand what gender and age groups were most affected and break the trends down by state at the end for user
exploration. There were a total of 601,124 COVID-19 deaths in the United States from January 1st, 2020 until July 17th, 2021.
As you can see in the chart below, COVID-19 has affected males more than females.

Page 1

After understanding which gender was affected the most, I wanted to break the data out into age groups.
As you can see males are affected the most for all age groups, except the 85+ age group.
Also, there is a huge jump in deaths in the 50-64 age group for both males and females.

Page 2

Finally on this page I wanted to allow the user to explore the death trends per state.
Select the state you want to look at and you can see the trends per gender and age group.
This allows you to better understand the trends in states with a predominantly older population
compared to states with a younger population.
